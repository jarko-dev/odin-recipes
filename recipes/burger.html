Burger

Description

This burger recipe is a classic and delicious way to enjoy homemade burgers. With a juicy patty, fresh toppings, and a soft bun, it's sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Your choice of toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.)

Steps:

  1. Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix until just combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on each bun and adding your choice of toppings.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade burgers!
