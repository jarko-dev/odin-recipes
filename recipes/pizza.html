Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe is a classic and delicious way to enjoy homemade pizza. With a crispy crust, flavorful sauce, and gooey cheese, it's sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Toppings:

  1. 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  2. 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  3. 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  3. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a greased pizza pan.
  8. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, and then add sliced pepperoni on top.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
